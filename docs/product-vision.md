# Product Vision

## What This Is
Two Globes is a reflective iOS app for capturing lived fragments and noticing patterns across two emotional spaces: green for energy, wins, and aliveness; red for friction, heaviness, and stuckness.

## What It Refuses To Be
- Not a therapist
- Not a coach
- Not a streak machine
- Not a guilt engine
- Not a feed of generic advice

## Pattern Types
- `Asymmetry`: one globe grows louder, richer, or more detailed than the other.
- `Contradiction`: entries point in opposing directions that the user may not have noticed.
- `Drift`: vocabulary, tone, or recurring themes shift over time.

## Core Principles
- Never prescribes.
- Every claim is traceable to captured material.
- No streaks, no guilt, no shame loops.
- Export is one tap.

## Daily Loop
- `Capture`: record or enter a fragment.
- `Sort`: review the proposed globe assignment.
- `Tag`: confirm, edit, or remove pointers.
- `Notice`: return later to see one restrained pattern at most.
